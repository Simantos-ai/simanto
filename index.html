BATTLE ROYALE

Fortnite Battle Royale is a game mode where 100 players fight to be the last player or team remaining.
Players must search for weapons, collect resources and harvest materials to battle others and claim that victory royale

CREATIVE

In this mode, players can design and build their own maps, game modes, and experiences. It gives players the freedom to create custom content and share it with others.
In addition to thier latest UEFN update, creative is constantly expanding and the possibilites are becoming endless.

Save The World

Save the World is a mode in Fortnite where players work together to complete missions and fight off zombie-like creatures. You gather resources, build defenses, and create weapons to protect your base from waves of enemies.
